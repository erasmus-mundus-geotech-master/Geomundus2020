GeoMundus 2020

PARTICIPANT REGISTRATION

The registration for the conference is now open! Welcome to Geomundus 12th edition. We will be thrilled to have your presence (either physically or remote) at this international event!

The coference will conduct in two phases as a PreConference Event on Thursday 26th November 2020 and Conference on Friday 27th and Saturday 28th November 2020 at Institute for Geoinformatics, University of Muenster. Please fill your application in below.

Please note that GeoMundus does not provide invitations and does not support Visa Processes. Due to the current ongoing scenario of Covid-19, physical attendees must ensure they comply with local regulations including social distancing and appropriate precautions. Physical spaces are limited due to current Covid-19 social distancing measures, if restrictions are eased closer to the date, GeoMundus will open more spaces for physical attendance. The situation is being monitored and constantly updated.
